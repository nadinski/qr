Shar-Shara — Seamless Digital Menu Experience

Extending the brand's elegant duality into the digital realm, I designed a dynamic QR menu that prioritizes intuitive usability and modern engagement.

Building on the established print identity, this digital menu translates the restaurant's fusion of Eastern and European cuisines into a fluid, mobile-first experience. The interface features graceful animations that guide the eye and enrich the interaction without compromising on clarity.

Special attention was paid to universal accessibility, ensuring the navigation is simple and logical for guests of all ages and technical proficiencies. Large, tappable elements, a clear typographic hierarchy, and an uncluttered layout make browsing effortless and enjoyable.

The result is a fast, elegant, and universally accessible digital menu that delivers a hassle-free experience from any smartphone or tablet — turning every table into a curated moment of discovery.
